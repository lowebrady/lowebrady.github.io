// HOW I WORK

My Design Process

01

Discover

User interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis to deeply understand the problem space before any design begins.

02

Define

Synthesize findings through affinity mapping, personas, and empathy maps to align on the real user needs.

03

Design

Wireframes, information architecture, and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma — tested early and often.

04

Evaluate

Heuristic evaluation, usability testing, SUS scoring, and accessibility audits to validate and iterate.
